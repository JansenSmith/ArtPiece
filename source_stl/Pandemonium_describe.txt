Project: Pandemonium.hfp
Print at 100% infill with a layer height of 0.08mm with a base layer of 0.08mm

 The Model is 200x131.95mm in size
 You may print at higher layer heights below the Min Depth of 0.16mm
 The Max Depth is 6mm and the Actual Depth is 6.1mm

Filaments Used:
	PLA Hatchbox Trans Yellow Transmission Distance: 60
	PLA Hatchbox Trans Orange Transmission Distance: 60
	PLA Hatchbox Trans Red Transmission Distance: 60
	PLA Hatchbox Trans Black Transmission Distance: 60
This print uses 4 unique filaments

Swap Instructions:
	Start with Trans Yellow
	At layer #15 (1.2mm) swap to Trans Orange
	At layer #33 (2.64mm) swap to Trans Red
	At layer #64 (5.12mm) swap to Trans Black for the rest.
